Ingredients

This cheesecake recipe is simple and delicious! Follow these ingredients to make the perfect cheesecake!

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g melted butter
  • 500g cream cheese
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 150g sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 200g Fresh berries (for topping)
