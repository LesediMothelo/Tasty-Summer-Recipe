Method

This cheesecake recipe is simple and delicious! Follow these steps to make a perfect cheesecake:

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).

2. In a bowl, mix the crushed biscuits with melted butter.

3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.

4. In another bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.

5. Add sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract, mixing well.

6. Pour the mixture over the biscuit base.

7. Bake for 45-50 minutes until set!

8. Let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

9. Top with fresh berries before serving and enjoy!
